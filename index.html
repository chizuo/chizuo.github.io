An Action Item is an activity that must be performed to facilitate the resolution of an Issue. Unlike Tasks, Action Items are not decomposed.

Go to Decisions »

A Decision is a conclusion reached by an individual or a group charged with making the Decision. Like an Issue, a Decision or lack thereof, will affect the successful completion of a Task. As such, Decisions are associated with Task.

Go to Issues »

An Issue is a concern, problem or an outstanding question that prevents a Resource from completing a Task. As such, Issues are associated with Task. More than one Issue can be associated with a Task and sometimes an issue may affect multiple tasks.


Go to Resources »

A Resource is person, employee or contractor that can be assigned to a Task.

Go to Risks »

A Risk is an uncertain event or condition that if it occurs, has a positive or negative effect on the project. Risks may be associated with one or more Deliverables or one or more Tasks.

Go to Tasks »

All Tasks that comprise the project must be completed for the successful delivery of the project.
